# 🧠 **TrustLens**  
### _Verify the Truth in a World Full of Fakes — Powered by AI, Polygon & Filecoin_

---

## 🚀 **Overview**

**TrustLens** is a decentralized platform that helps users verify the **authenticity of digital content** — such as images, videos, and news posts — using **AI and blockchain**.

Creators and journalists can **timestamp and register** their original content on the **Polygon blockchain**, while the actual media files are stored securely on **Filecoin**, a decentralized storage network.

Anyone can then **upload or paste content** into TrustLens to verify if it’s real or fake — within seconds.

---

## 💡 **Why TrustLens?**

In today’s world, misinformation spreads faster than truth — with **AI-generated images**, **deepfake videos**, and **fake news** everywhere.  
TrustLens gives people a **transparent and verifiable** way to check whether content is **authentic**, empowering creators, journalists, and the public to fight misinformation.

> 🕵️‍♀️ *“Verify before you believe.”*

---

## 🧩 **How It Works (Simple Steps)**

### 1️⃣ Upload or Register Content
- A creator uploads an **image**, **video**, or **text**.
- The system creates a **unique digital fingerprint (hash)** of the content.
- This hash is stored on the **Polygon blockchain** along with:
  - Creator’s **wallet address**
  - **Timestamp** of upload
- The **media file itself** is stored securely on **Filecoin**.

---

### 2️⃣ Verification Process
- Anyone can upload or paste a URL to verify authenticity.
- The system re-generates the hash and checks it on the blockchain.

| Result | Meaning |
|:--|:--|
| ✅ **Verified Original** | The hash matches the blockchain record |
| ⚠️ **Possible Fake or Altered** | No match found — content may be modified |

---

### 3️⃣ AI Deepfake Detection *(Optional Feature)*
- Suspicious videos or images are analyzed by a **deepfake detection model** built in **Python (CNN + OpenCV)**.  
- The AI gives a **“real vs fake” confidence score** to show how authentic the content appears.
